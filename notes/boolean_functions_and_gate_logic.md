### NAND

Definition: (X NAND Y) = NOT(X AND Y)

<img alt="NAND gate" src="https://i.imgur.com/gLKYxiV.png" />

#### NOT represented with NAND

NOT(x) = (x NAND x)

#### AND represented with NAND

(x AND y) = NOT(x NAND y)
